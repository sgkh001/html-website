BlueBay Beach!

Surrounded by Filao trees in a semi-circle, the beach has deep, azure waters, sparkling sandy shores and lively and vibrant people. There are plethora of activities like like swimming, diving, snorkeling, rowing, and fishing if you are looking at some fun and adventure. The marvelous turquoise water accompanied by the luxuriant greenery around make for a spectacular view.

Famous for: Water activities and sports – yachting and windsurfing to be precise. Also known to be a perfect destination for honeymoon and family trips, Blue Bay Beach offers many couple-y experiences like banana ride, water scooters, parasailing and such

Key attraction/s: A peek into world’s one of the biggest marine park, The Blue Bay Marine Park, certainly comes as a perk.
