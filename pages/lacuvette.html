La Cuvette Beach!

Situated near the town of Grand Bay in the northwest of Mauritius, La Cuvette Beach is a small beach as compared to the other beaches in Mauritius, but an extremely beautiful one, nevertheless. It is haven for those who love tranquil places minus all the hoopla related to heavy crowds. The azure, crystal clear waters coupled with the dazzling sand and boulders, and attractive cliffs, make for a perfect place for solitude seeking nature lovers. So, this is one of the quite beaches in Mauritius you must visit!

Famous for: Tranquility. A visit to this beach promises a refreshing pleasure of being surrounded in the peace, serenity and beauty. It is also known for its perfect currents to indulge in sailing and yachting.

Key attractions: The luxurious Royal Palm Beach Resort nearby is a great crowd puller.
